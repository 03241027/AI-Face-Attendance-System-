💡 TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DO:
  ✓ Use bright, even lighting (daylight or 500+ lux)
  ✓ Face camera directly
  ✓ Keep face in center of frame
  ✓ Hold position for 2-3 seconds
  ✓ Ensure clear view of face
  ✓ Use same camera consistently

DON'T:
  ✗ Use poor/side lighting
  ✗ Face at extreme angles
  ✗ Wear large sunglasses
  ✗ Cover face with objects
  ✗ Move quickly
  ✗ Use different cameras frequently
