================================================================================
                        🔍 7 MAIN REASONS
================================================================================

1. Threshold Too Strict
   └─ Current < 35 rejects even good matches
   └─ FIX: Lower to < 50

2. Not Enough Training Images
   └─ Only 1-2 images, needs 20-30
   └─ FIX: Recapture more images

3. Lighting Different
   └─ Train in bright light, camera in dark
   └─ FIX: Train in varied lighting

4. Wrong Angle
   └─ Train only frontal, camera is profile
   └─ FIX: Train multiple angles

5. Bad Image Quality
   └─ Blurry or dark training images
   └─ FIX: Delete bad images, recapture

6. Wrong Distance
   └─ Train close, camera far (or vice versa)
   └─ FIX: Train at multiple distances

7. Preprocessing Mismatch
   └─ Training ≠ camera processing
   └─ FIX: Use dlib instead
