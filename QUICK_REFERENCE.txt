🎥 DATA COLLECTION TIPS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
✅ DO:
  • Use bright, even lighting
  • Face camera directly
  • Capture 20-30 diverse images
  • Vary angles slightly
  • Keep neutral expression
  • Ensure 30-60cm distance

❌ DON'T:
  • Use poor/side lighting
  • Face at extreme angles
  • Use less than 20 images
  • Wear sunglasses/hats
  • Have obstructed face
  • Stand too far away
